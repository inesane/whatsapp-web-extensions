# Privacy Policy — WhatsApp Group Compare

**Last updated:** March 16, 2026

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All data processed by the extension remains entirely within your browser.

## How It Works

The extension reads group member information directly from the WhatsApp Web page in your browser. This data is stored locally in your browser's extension storage so you can compare two groups without losing data between captures. No data is sent to any external server.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: Used to access the WhatsApp Web tab to read group information.
- **storage**: Used to save captured group data locally in your browser.
- **scripting**: Used to inject the content script into WhatsApp Web.
